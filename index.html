GCSE Revision Tips

General Revision Tips

  • Start revising early and make a revision timetable.
  • Break study time into short sessions with breaks in between.
  • Practice past papers to get used to exam style questions.
  • Use active recall and test yourself regularly.
  • Stay hydrated and get plenty of sleep.

Subject-Specific Tips

  • Maths: Practice problems daily, focus on formulas and problem-solving methods.
  • English: Read texts carefully, learn quotes, and practice writing essays.
  • Science: Understand concepts, memorize key terms, and do hands-on experiments if possible.
  • History: Make timelines, learn key dates, and practice essay writing.
  • Languages: Practice speaking, listening, and vocabulary daily.

Time Management

  • Set specific goals for each revision session.
  • Use timers like the Pomodoro technique (25 minutes study, 5 minutes break).
  • Prioritize harder subjects when you’re most alert.
  • Avoid multitasking — focus on one topic at a time.
  • Review your timetable weekly and adjust as needed.
